Mission Statement

The “Palette & Plate” Gallery is an inclusive for the creatives to have a space where they can showcase their talent in the form of a dish. A delicious dining experience that is made for artists and by artists. Our mission is to provide those with an appreciation for creative dining a safe, warm place to explore food influenced by local artists.
